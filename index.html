Industrial Safety Equipment Tracker

Replaced $30,000/year of licensed software with a system built on the Microsoft 365 the company already paid for.

The problem

A petrochemical plant tracked ~360 critical safety assets — extinguishers, breathing apparatus, safety showers, hydrants, lock-out boxes — by hand and through a $30K/year third-party app. No live oversight, no real audit trail, and easy to sign off an inspection without physically checking the equipment.

The system

A field app captures every inspection with mandatory photo proof, writing to a single SharePoint database with an automatic audit trail. Automated workflows run the governance — daily health summaries, compliance checks against the audit schedule, and a 72-hour escalation that pushes any unresolved deficiency up to management. A live dashboard gives leadership view-only oversight.

TR-01 — Predictive Column Monitoring

An early-warning layer that catches slow process drift days before it becomes an upset — the trend no single shift stays long enough to see.

The problem

A control room's DCS owns the immediate — alarms, trips, second-by-second response. But fouling, drift, and creeping instability develop over weeks, while operators and supervisors rotate every 8–12 hours. Nobody stays on shift long enough to own the long view, so slow problems arrive as sudden surprises.

The system

A stewardship layer that sits above the control system. It watches each instrument's trend and the relationships between them, then flags developing failure signatures — reboiler fouling, column flooding, feed instability — with a predicted time-to-consequence and a suggested mitigation, while there's still time to plan rather than react.

MERIDIAN — PSX Equity & Portfolio Analytics

Turns a portfolio of stocks into a plain-English read on return, risk, and drawdown — with a guided assistant that explains the data and refuses to give advice.

The problem

Retail investors drown in price noise and jargon. Most tools either dump raw numbers or push recommendations. The real need: see what a holding or portfolio actually did — how it grew, how much it swung, how deep it fell — and understand it without a finance degree.

The system

An equity-research-style tool that computes the metrics that matter — annualised return, volatility, max drawdown, Sharpe, and beta to the market — across a selectable portfolio, with growth, drawdown, and risk-return exhibits. A built-in assistant explains every number in plain language and deliberately refuses buy/sell advice.
